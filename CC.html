Climate Change



What's The Problem?

Climate change impacts the temperature and weather of the world we live in.
Temperature and weather changes today are happening more because of humans than nature.
There are several impacts on the Earth because of climate change.
Deserts are expanding, heatwaves and wildfires are becoming more common.
Increased warming in the Arctic is melting permafrost, glacial retreat, and sea ice loss.
Higher temperatures also cause instense storms, droughts, and more extreme weather.
This also changes mountains, coral reefs, and forces arctic animals to relocate or go extinct.

How Can I Help?

There are many ways to help stop this crisis.
You can turn of the lights when not in a room, you can take quicker showers.
When you are going to a close destination, try walking or going on a bicycle.
You can also use electric cars.
