Pollution



What's The Problem?

Environmental pollution, Water pollution, and Air pollution, all affect the Earth.
Pollution makes our planet more and more uninhabitable.
Pollution can travel very far.
Some chemicals from pollution traveled all the way to Antarctica.
There is some much pollution, that, even in the ocean, there are many large garbage patches, one being almost the size of Texas!
All living things, from one-celled organisms to blue whales depend on water, air, and other natural resources.
Pollution is destroying these things and endangering all life on earth.
Pollution also makes the beautiful "blue marble" look worse.

How Can I Help?

There are many ways to help prevent pollution.
Use recyclables, and reusable items.
Try using bicycles, electric cars, or walking to prevent air pollution, which can also cause climate change.
You can use special browsers like ocg.org that pick up trash when you search.
Tell many people about it, so they can stop pollution too.
